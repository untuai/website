Untu HotelBot H1

Product Code: UTH-001  |  Category: Autonomous Service Robot

An autonomous service robot designed to enhance guest experiences in hotels through automated delivery, concierge guidance, and 24/7 information services. Built for reliability, seamlessly integrating with your existing infrastructure.

Key Features

  • ▶ Autonomous room service delivery with SLAM & V-SLAM navigation — no floor modification required.
  • ▶ Native integration with Hotel Management Systems (HMS) and elevator controls for seamless multi-floor operations.
  • ▶ Interactive touchscreen and voice interface for intuitive guest engagement and real-time status updates.
