Capabilities

Built for the demands of real hotel operations.

Autonomous SLAM Navigation

Self-mapping and self-locating. The robot builds and updates its own floor plan in real time — no QR codes, no magnetic strips, no permanent markings.

360° Obstacle Avoidance

Multi-layer sensors detect guests, luggage carts, and open doors. The robot reroutes in real time — never stops, never gets confused.

Multi-Floor + Elevator Integration

Calls and rides the elevator independently. Delivers to any floor of your property without human assistance at any point in the journey.

24/7 Operation

9–12 hour battery life with automatic return to charging dock. The robot is ready for the next delivery before your staff clock in for the morning shift.

Guest Notifications

Guests receive real-time SMS or in-app updates on delivery status. The robot knocks, announces arrival, and waits — just like a well-trained team member.

HMS Integration

Reads orders directly from your hotel management system. No separate app, no manual handoffs — delivery triggers automatically when an order is placed.
