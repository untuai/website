Why Untu AI

Intelligent automation built for hospitality.

Hospitality is a human business — but not every task requires a human. Untu AI builds autonomous service robots that handle the repetitive, the logistical, and the routine, so your staff can focus on what truly matters: the guest relationship.

Our robots integrate seamlessly with your existing hotel management systems, operate across multiple floors via elevator integration, and deliver consistent, reliable service around the clock.
