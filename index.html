Capabilities

Built for the demands of real hotel operations.

Autonomous SLAM Navigation

Self-maps your property. Adapts to layout changes in real time. No QR codes, no magnetic strips.

360° Obstacle Avoidance

Detects guests, carts and open doors. Reroutes automatically — never stops, never gets stuck.

Multi-Floor + Elevator Control

Calls and rides the elevator independently. Delivers to any floor without human assistance.

24/7 Operation

9–12 hour battery, auto-return to charging dock. Ready before your morning staff arrives.

Guest Notifications

Real-time SMS or in-app updates. Knocks, announces arrival, and waits — just like a team member.

HMS Integration

Reads orders directly from your PMS. Delivery triggers automatically the moment an order is placed.
